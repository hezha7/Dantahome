About Danta Home

Danta Home, established on January 5, 2020, is a leading provider of home appliances in Iraq-Kurdistan. Located at New Sulaimani Street, Light Building, 2nd Floor, we specialize in sourcing high-quality electronic home appliances from renowned global companies. Offering a wide range of products, including televisions and dishwashers, we cater to the diverse needs of our customers. Our mission is to bring the latest and most efficient home solutions to our community, ensuring satisfaction and convenience in every household. Our dedication to quality and customer service sets us apart in the competitive market of home appliances.
